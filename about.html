Everything is Art

From digital work to full-sized dolls, Vic creates using any medium she can get her hands on. Collage, watercolor, oils, and scraps from the garbage are all fair game.

Vic is a 19 year old student from Pennsylvania with a passion for science and creation. Her creative muses include science, music, sexuality, and self-expression.
